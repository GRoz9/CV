Spotify to YouTube playlist converter

I've decided to do this project for one reason, which was to share my playlist with my friend which uses YouTube instead of Spotify. This allows me to update the YouTube playlist so me and my friend can share our songs together

While creating the program, I faced several challenges, one of which was collecting more than 100 songs using an API. Each API call had a limit of 100 songs, and I was initially stumped by this issue as I was new to using APIs. However, after breaking down the problem into smaller parts, I discovered a solution. I used a loop to gather multiple pages of data and concatenate the results to obtain a list of all the songs I needed. This not only solved the problem but also helped me gain a better understanding of breaking down complex issues into smaller, manageable tasks. Overcoming this obstacle gave me great joy and further inspired me to keep learning new concepts.
